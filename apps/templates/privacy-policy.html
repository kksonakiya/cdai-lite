Privacy Policy

1. Offline by Design

This app is a fully offline tool. It does not require or initiate any connection to external servers, APIs, or databases.

2. No User Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users. There are no analytics, cookies, or background network activity.

3. No Accounts, No Ads

This application does not use authentication. There are no user accounts, no passwords, and no identity tracking. Additionally, the app displays no advertisements and includes no ad-related scripts or services.

4. Model Downloads

While we provide external links to download models (e.g., from Civitai), the app does not download or install anything automatically. All downloads are initiated manually by the user.

5. Full Privacy Assurance

Since this app has no internet communication, your data remains entirely private and under your control.
