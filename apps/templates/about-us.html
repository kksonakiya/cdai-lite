About CDAI lite

CDAI lite is a lightweight, offline image generation and comparison app developed by CodeJana AI Pvt Ltd. It's designed to help AI artists, creators, and enthusiasts quickly compare Civitai models, including both checkpoint and LoRA models — all without needing to go online.

🔍 Our Mission

Our mission is to give users a fast, easy, and private way to explore and compare image generation models with different LoRA combinations — right from their local machine.

💡 How It Started

CDAI lite was born out of necessity — I, K K Sonakiya, found it difficult to find the right Civitai models for use in our main app, ChatDocsAI.com. That frustration turned into a simple, powerful offline solution now known as CDAI lite.

🎯 Who It's For

CDAI lite is perfect for:

  • AI artists and enthusiasts
  • Print-on-demand businesses
  • AI image entrepreneurs
  • Anyone curious about AI-generated visuals

🚀 What's Next

We're actively building more features into CDAI lite — all for free. Planned features include:

  • Image upscaling
  • Video generation support
  • Support for better and more diverse models
